FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Is Pilates suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! Pilates is for everyone regardless of fitness level. Our instructors modify exercises to suit each individual's abilities and gradually increase the challenge as you progress.

What should I wear to Pilates?

Wear comfortable, form-fitting clothing that allows you to move freely. Avoid baggy clothes as instructors need to see your form. Pilates is typically done barefoot or with grip socks.

How often should I do Pilates?

For best results, we recommend 2-3 sessions per week. Many clients combine Pilates with other forms of exercise for a balanced fitness routine.

Can Pilates help with back pain?

Yes! Pilates is excellent for improving core strength and spinal alignment, which can help alleviate back pain. Many physical therapists recommend Pilates for rehabilitation.

What's the difference between mat and reformer Pilates?

Mat Pilates uses body weight and small props for resistance, while reformer Pilates uses a specialized machine with springs for added resistance and support. Both are effective - reformers allow for more variations.
