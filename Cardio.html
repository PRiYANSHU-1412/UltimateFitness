FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How often should I do cardio?

For general health, aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate cardio or 75 minutes of vigorous cardio per week. Our trainers can help you create a schedule that fits your goals and fitness level.

Is cardio or strength training better for weight loss?

Both are important! Cardio burns more calories during the workout, while strength training builds muscle that boosts metabolism. Our programs combine both for optimal results.

What's the best cardio machine for beginners?

The elliptical is great for beginners as it's low-impact but provides a full-body workout. Our trainers will help you find the right equipment based on your fitness level and goals.

How can I make cardio less boring?

Try our group classes, listen to music or podcasts, vary your workouts, or use interval training to keep things interesting. Our coaches specialize in making cardio engaging and fun!
