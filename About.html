HOW IT ALL BEGAN

Founded in 2015, Ultimate Fitness started as a small neighborhood gym with just 500 square feet of space and a handful of dedicated members. What began as a passion project quickly grew into one of the most respected fitness centers in the region.

Our founder, Priyanshu Srivastava, saw a need for a fitness facility that combined world-class equipment with personalized attention and a welcoming community atmosphere. He believed that everyone - regardless of their fitness level - deserved access to quality training and support.

Today, our 10,000 square foot facility serves over 2,000 members with state-of-the-art equipment, diverse class offerings, and a team of certified trainers. But despite our growth, we've never lost sight of our original mission: to help people become the strongest, healthiest versions of themselves.

Priyanshu Srivastava

Founder & CEO of Ultimate Fitness

Priyanshu has dedicated his life to helping others achieve their health and wellness goals. A certified personal trainer and nutrition specialist, he competed in bodybuilding competitions before shifting his focus to building Ultimate Fitness.

"Fitness changed my life, and I wanted to create a space where others could experience that same transformation. At Ultimate Fitness, we're not just about building muscles - we're about building confidence, health, and community."
